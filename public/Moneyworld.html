MoneyWorld is a podcast created under the organization known as Cashonomics, founded by Arnav Shah and Dhwani Das with a mission to educate the world about financial literacy, no matter their age or status. We will bring on guest speakers and allow them to share their opinions on the business and financial world along with personal episodes where we talk about current events occurring in the world.
